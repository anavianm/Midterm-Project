Chocola

Toy Poodle

Age: 2 years

Likes: People, metal bowls, and refrigerators

Dislikes: Darkness

Favorite Brand of Dog Food: Natural Balance

Chocola is a super friendly dog who loves meeting new people. She loves going on walks and letting other people pet her. Dambo

Dislikes: Objects that don’t fit in her mouth

Mogu
